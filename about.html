About the Mini Store

Empowering student entrepreneurship and peer creativity on campus.

storefront Our Mission

The NSBM Mini Store is a student-centric campus marketplace designed to empower student entrepreneurship and showcase peer creativity. Run by the Student Union, we curate official faculty merchandise, custom stationery, and handcrafted student works, helping creators grow their micro-businesses on campus.

Every single purchase directly supports local student artisans, student bakers, and creative digital artists, fostering a collaborative ecosystem of campus commerce.

volunteer_activism Supporting Student Creators

We believe in fostering practical skills along with academic learning. By providing student creators a dynamic platform to register and sell their crafts, designs, and baked items, the Mini Store serves as an incubator for the next generation of academic entrepreneurs.
